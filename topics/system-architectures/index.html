Monolith vs Microservices Decision Framework

üì¶ Monolithic Architecture

When to Choose:
  • Small team (< 10 developers)
  • Early-stage startup/MVP
  • Simple domain model
  • Fast time-to-market needed
  • Limited DevOps expertise
Advantages:
  • Simple deployment
  • Easy debugging
  • Lower infrastructure cost
  • Faster initial development
Challenges:
  • Scaling limitations
  • Technology lock-in
  • Deployment risk

üîß Microservices Architecture

When to Choose:
  • Large team (10+ developers)
  • Complex domain boundaries
  • Different scaling needs per service
  • Multiple technology stacks
  • Strong DevOps capabilities
Advantages:
  • Independent scaling
  • Technology flexibility
  • Fault isolation
  • Team autonomy
Challenges:
  • Distributed complexity
  • Network latency
  • Operational overhead
